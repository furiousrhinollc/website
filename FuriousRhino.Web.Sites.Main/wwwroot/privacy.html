Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Overview

Furious Rhino is committed to protecting user privacy and minimizing data collection. This app does not collect or store personal information from users except for anonymized metrics automatically provided by Apple and Google for analytics, crash reporting, and platform support. Any platform-provided metrics we receive are used only to improve app stability and performance and are not linked to individual user identities.

Why we publish a privacy policy

Both Apple and Google require apps to disclose their privacy practices and to provide a privacy policy URL in the store listing. Even apps that do not collect personal data must publish a clear privacy policy explaining their practices.

What we collect

  • No personal data — We do not request, collect, or transmit names, email addresses, contacts, precise location, photos, or other personally identifiable information.
  • Anonymized platform metrics — The only data transmitted off-device are anonymized analytics and diagnostic metrics supplied by Apple and Google (for example: crash reports, aggregated usage counts, and performance telemetry). These are used solely for product improvement and troubleshooting.

How we use data

  • Analytics and quality — Anonymized metrics help us monitor crashes, measure feature usage in aggregate, and prioritize bug fixes.
  • Support and billing — When required by the platform, limited information necessary for App Store or Play billing, subscription management, or platform support may be shared with Apple or Google only. We do not share personal data with other parties for these purposes.

Sharing and third parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties. We do not integrate advertising networks, data brokers, or third-party analytics vendors that collect user-identifying data. Platform providers (Apple and Google) may receive data necessary for their services (for example, billing and aggregated crash reporting); such sharing is governed by their platform policies.

Data retention and deletion

Anonymized metrics are retained only as long as necessary to support app quality and stability improvements. Because we do not collect personal profiles, there is no personal profile to delete. If you believe the app has transmitted data that could identify you, contact us and we will investigate and take appropriate action.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we handle. No transmission over the internet or storage system is completely secure; platform providers also maintain security controls for their services.

Children

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Material changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

Contact

For questions about this policy or privacy practices, email: support@furiousrhino.com.

Summary: No personal data is collected or sold; only anonymized Apple/Google metrics are used for analytics, crash reporting, and support; platform providers may receive platform-required information for billing/support.
